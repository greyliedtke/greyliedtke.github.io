Grey

Hello! My name is a color! I was born and raised in the land of 10,000 lakes, Minnesota. I attended Minnetonka High and passed time with doing anything active. I chased sunshine, waves and hands on experience and received a bachelors of science in mechanical engineering from California Polytechnic State University. I now am a programming engineer at Automated Solutions Group, a building automation company that controls energy systems for the buildings of Silicon Valley.

In my free time, I enjoy trail running, slack lining, and anything active. When I am not working, I am usually working. On personal projects! I enjoy 3d printing, wood working, coding, anything crafty. I highly value the resources we all have at our fingertips. Whether that be I can search the web to learn anything, or click a screen and have a 3d printer at my house in two days. We live in a marvelous world of technology and I am passionate about learning and utilizing all it has to offer!

Thank you for visiting and reading this brief synopsis on my life. If you would like to talk more, send a joke, riddle or funny story, email me below!
